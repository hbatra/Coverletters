Who we are

Cover Letters was founded by Henna and Kadejha, current students at the University of Leicester. After years of applying for insight days, internships, student jobs and of course, completing the personal statement for UCAS, we understand the time consuming process of applications.

We have had extensive coaching from various careers advisors, so be rest assured that the helpers at cover letters are fully qualified to fulfil your job application needs.

Why we do this

Having parents from a minority background, our helpers understand that the recruitment process in today's job market can seem daunting and writing a lengthy cover letter may not be a main priority. We openly welcome everyone to contact us for help with matters like these, and rest assured we are happy to respond to any questions that you may have in these new experiences you are trying to gain.
